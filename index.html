About Us

Leaving a positive footprint through transformative solutions.

Footprints Consultancy sets out to be a leading consulting firm providing countless solutions designed for clients. We focus on solving challenges faced by our clients, delivering transformation while leaving a positive footprint.

Footprints offers a comprehensive suite of legal, governance and advisory services delivered by seasoned professionals with deep industry experience. Beyond standard transactions, we help organisations design robust governance frameworks, manage disputes and secure compliance. Our core services include board secretarial functions, banking and finance advisory, land and conveyancing, employment and labour law, corporate and commercial law, investment law, real estate and construction, oil, gas & mining, intellectual property, insurance and executive training programmes.

Each service area is tailored to client needs: our board secretarial team organises meetings, prepares agendas and minutes, maintains records and files statutory returns; our banking and finance practice handles credit disputes, restructurings, due diligence and drafting of loan documents; land and conveyancing specialists navigate property development, project finance and cross‑border lending; employment experts advise on contracts, policies and retrenchment matters; and our corporate and commercial lawyers manage everything from public listings and joint ventures to mergers, acquisitions and contractual negotiations.

We also provide guidance on investment laws for incentives offered by the Tanzania Investment Centre and Zanzibar Investment Promotion Authority, advise on complex real estate and construction projects, support clients in the extractive industries, protect intangible assets through intellectual property services and manage insurance contracts and claims. Our experienced trainers deliver bespoke programmes in corporate governance, risk management, customer care and relationship skills, leadership and self‑development, financial and investment principles and team building, helping organisations build empowered boards and high‑performing teams.

Joseph Tadayo is an advocate and corporate law consultant with extensive experience of over 30 years in areas of banking, corporate, commercial, intellectual property rights, mining, energy and projects.

He is highly sought after for both government and commercial litigation and arbitration, as well as drafting and reviewing various legal instruments. Throughout his career he has maintained a brand of solid legal knowledge and a practical sense of how to get along, making TanAfrica Law a result‑oriented firm.

Apart from his extensive corporate experience Joseph previously served the public as a Member of Parliament and was a member of the Parliamentary Legal and Constitutional Affairs Committee, the Parliamentary Immunities, Powers and Privileges Committee and the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Local Authorities Accounts (LAAC).
